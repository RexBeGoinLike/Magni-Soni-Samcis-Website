"OPM RESONANCE: A SONIC JOURNEY THROUGH TIME"

December 22, 2023

Under the leadership of SLU Magni Soni SAMCIS, we invite you to our upcoming concert, "OPM Resonance: A Sonic Journey Through Time." Join us in revisiting the evolution of Filipino music as our performers interpret OPM hits from the 1980s to the present. The concert will take place at Devesse AVR (D1001) on November 22, starting at 4:30 PM. Tickets are only 35 pesos and can be purchased on November 21-22 at Devesse Lobby from 9:00 AM to 6:30 PM. Payment will be collected upon ticket retrieval. Come and show your love and continuous support for Filipino music.
