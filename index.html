WELCOME TO MAGNI SONI SAMCIS

We are the Sonics of Magni Soni SAMCIS, and we invite you to immerse yourself in the harmonies created by our group. Established in 2010 as the SAMCIS Chorale, we adopted our current identity in 2023 at the Saint Louis University Maryheights Campus in Bakakeng, Baguio City. Our organization is dedicated to providing talented individuals at Saint Louis University with opportunities, with a primary focus on the music industry. Don't miss the chance to display your skills – seize the opportunity!
